## Summary

1. **User creates order** through 8-step wizard with all case details and documents
2. **System processes order** - validates, creates payment intent, saves to database
3. **Claude AI automatically generates** motion draft using superprompt template merged with case data
4. **Admin reviews** in dashboard "Ready to Approve" tab with full Claude chat access
5. **Admin approves** with one-click, generating PDF and notifying client
6. **Client receives** motion and can request one free revision if needed
7. **Order completes** and moves to "Completed" tab

The Claude AI is deeply integrated at the core of the workflow, handling the heavy lifting of motion drafting while admins maintain quality control through the approval process.
